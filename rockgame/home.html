Once upon a future, there were three primordial elements : the RHOMBUS, the RECTANGLE, and the DOUBLE-CIRCLE

The RHOMBUS, asymmetrical and unpredictable, could cut through the DOUBLE-CIRCLE

The RECTANGLE, still and solid, could inglobate the RHOMBUS

The DOUBLE-CIRCLE, dynamic and fast, could hypnotize the RECTANGLE

Those who won became YELLOW, the color of intelligence...
